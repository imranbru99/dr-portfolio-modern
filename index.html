About Me

A Life Devoted to
Healing & Discovery

With over two decades of clinical experience and research, I specialize in interventional cardiology, cardiac imaging, and preventive heart health. My practice bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and compassionate patient care.

"Medicine is not just a science — it is the art of understanding the human condition."

Trained at Harvard Medical School and fellowship-trained at the Mayo Clinic, I've dedicated my career to advancing cardiovascular medicine through rigorous research, innovative therapies, and teaching the next generation of physicians.

Interventional Cardiology Cardiac Imaging Clinical Research Heart Failure Echocardiography Medical Education Preventive Cardiology Electrophysiology
Areas of Expertise

Clinical Specializations

01
Interventional Cardiology

Advanced catheter-based procedures for diagnosing and treating coronary artery disease, angioplasty, stenting, and structural heart interventions with precision and care.

02
Cardiac Imaging

Expert interpretation of echocardiography, cardiac MRI, CT angiography, and nuclear cardiology for comprehensive cardiovascular assessment and diagnosis.

03
Preventive Cardiology

Comprehensive risk stratification, lifestyle modification programs, and pharmacological strategies to prevent cardiovascular events in high-risk populations.

04
Clinical Research

Principal investigator in over 30 randomized controlled trials exploring novel therapeutics, biomarkers, and interventional techniques in cardiovascular medicine.

05
Medical Education

Dedicated educator and mentor, teaching cardiology fellows and medical students, serving on curriculum committees, and developing innovative educational programs.

06
Electrophysiology

Specialized management of complex arrhythmias including atrial fibrillation, ventricular tachycardia, and implantable device therapy for heart rhythm disorders.
